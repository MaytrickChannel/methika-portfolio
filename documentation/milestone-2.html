Content Strategy

Purpose

To showcase my front-end development skills and attract job opportunities, freelance clients, or collaborations by presenting my technical expertise and personality.My site will be one page, and will consist of the following sections:

Target Audience

  • Hiring Managers & Recruiters looking for front-end developers.
  • Freelance Clients seeking web development services.
  • Industry Peers interested in collaboration.

Tone

  • Personable and Approachable: Reflect your friendly and casual style, while still professional.
  • Clear and Direct: Highlight key skills and experience efficiently.

Home Page

  • Header: Logo, navigation links.
  • Hero Section: Intro headline and CTAs to portfolio and contact.

Content Sections

Welcome page

  • Intro headline, sub headline, and CTAs to my portfolio and contact.

About Me Page

  • Brief intro of my background and professional skills and experience. Also, highlight key roles and professional growth.

Portfolio Page

  • Intro text on my project approach.
  • Overview of my technical skills.
  • Images, titles, and brief descriptions with links to full case studies or demos.
  • Visual badges/icons showcasing my technical skills and tools used for projects.

Testimonials Page

  • Brief intro and testimonials from clients, old boss or colleagues.

Contact Page

  • Message inviting inquiries.
  • A simple contact form that is ready to be submitted after filling out the required fields.

This condensed structure keeps everything organized and easy to navigate, making your skills and projects stand out effectively.

The "Resume" button will link directly to the pdf download page.
